Happea

Onja Lyubomirsky, psychology professor at the University of California, defines happiness as, “the experience of joy, contentment, or positive well-being, combined with a sense that one’s life is good, meaningful, and worthwhile.” Is it as defined though? The happiness that we all feel doesn't seem so constrained. Any way we choose to define it though, we know it is dropping fast. We all feel that we are losing grip on our sentiments and drowning in chaos. Happiness seems to be running away and there is no sign of emotional stability. Leaving work to play cricket with children is no longer a satisfying choice neither is achieving as much as your father at half his age.
