Enter Happea

A portal aimed at understanding and measuring the phenomenon of happiness and peace in humans and therefore improve it. Psychologists from all over the world have made estimates of the biological causes of happiness and also conducted a lot of experiments to understand it but have failed to identify the differences caused by individual experiences, memories and cultural differences. The biggest reason behind the failure of these tests and lack of conclusive evidence is the inability to get genuine responses from the participants due to being aware of the test while giving it. Happea takes a different route to deal with this by categorising people in four categories based on age. This requires determining the sources of happiness and peace for each of these age groups, analyzing and deriving a method to gauge an individual’s happiness and implementing ways to enhance it.
